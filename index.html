About me

About me:

I'm Logan, a 28-year-old developer with a history of delivering elegant products and enjoyable experiences. As an engineer and even in my day-to-day life, I strive to make things better for others. I'm most excited by products with the potential to objectively and drastically improve lives, such as cryptocurrency.

I coded in college here and there (R, Stata, Mathematica, LaTeX), but my coding education didn't formally begin until 2015. I attended a coding bootcamp, which kindled my love for coding and helped propel me into the tech industry.

Today I'm an entrepreneur / developer / scientist / traveler with incorporated companies, published apps and academic papers, and dozens of countries under my belt. I intend to keep learning and developing with respect to this tetrad of identities.
